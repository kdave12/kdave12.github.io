About Me

I am pursuing my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering with a focus in Computational Engineering and Data Analytics at Carnegie Mellon. I pursued my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and a double major in Engineering and Public Policy (Engineering Economics) at Carnegie Mellon. I am interested in working at the intersection of engineering and data science. I am excited to develop well-connected, secure, and robust autonomous vehicle and Internet of Things (IoT) systems, and explore the macroeconomic and public policy implications of intelligent machines, smart cities and autonomous transportation on the current fabric of our society.
